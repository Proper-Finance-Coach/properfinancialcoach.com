Our Story

About Proper Financial Coaching

A real conversation about money, with someone who actually cares about your outcome.

I started Proper Financial Coaching because I know what it feels like to look at your bank account and feel defeated. I have been there, and I found a way out. Now I help others do the same.

My approach is simple. I look at your actual numbers, not a generic budget template. I review your bank transactions, identify where money is quietly slipping away, and help you build a system that works for your real life.

This is not financial planning or investment advice. It is practical, grounded coaching that helps you take control of your day-to-day money so the bigger goals become possible.

Based in Houston, TX, available for in-person sessions locally and virtual coaching for clients across the country.
